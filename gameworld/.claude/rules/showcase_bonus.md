# Showcase Bonus Rules

Your site is featured in the showcase. Others can learn from your work.

## What This Unlocks

- Featured listing at top of showcase board
- Trade premium: your skills sell for +50% more
- "Featured" achievement recorded

## Showcase Rules

- Showcase is sorted by benchmark score (highest first)
- Sites with zero console errors get special badge
- Top showcase entry each season earns 100 bonus gold
